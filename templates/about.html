About Us

Global Career Consultancy is a trusted overseas recruitment agency dedicated to connecting skilled and talented professionals with leading employers across Gulf countries and international markets. We specialize in providing manpower solutions for Construction, Technical, Hospitality, Driving, Office Administration, Healthcare, and various other sectors. Our goal is to create genuine overseas job opportunities while ensuring a smooth and transparent recruitment process for every candidate. With a strong network of direct clients and industry partners, we focus on quality placements, fast processing, and reliable support from application to departure.
